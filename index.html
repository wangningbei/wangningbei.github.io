I am currently a Professor at Nanjing University, Suzhou campus. My research mainly focuses on rendering and material appearance. I am also interested in 3D computer vision (e.g., surface reconstruction and relighting). The main target is to create a realistic 3D digital world, including creating (reconstructing and generating) 3D assets (geometries and materials) and rendering these assets realistically. Specifically,

  • New (non-neural, physically) material models.
  • Material + neural -> reconstruction + representation + generation.
  • Typical light transport problems (GI, sampling, path guiding, etc.).
  • Geometry/material reconstruction with NeRF/3DGS.
  • 3D objects (with materials) generation.
